## [0.1.0] - 2025-xx-xx
### Added
- Initial release.
- `Beam`, `Load`, `BoundaryCondition` classes.
- Static FEM solver (`solve_static`).
- Visualisation utilities (`plot_deflection`).
- Simply-supported and cantilever beam examples.
- Sphinx documentation.
